About TTS Document Converter

A free, accessible text-to-speech platform designed for students and educators.

Our Mission

TTS Document Converter is a free educational tool that transforms documents into natural-sounding audio. We're committed to making education more accessible by providing high-quality text-to-speech conversion powered by AWS Polly's advanced voice synthesis technology.

As a student-built platform, we understand the challenges of balancing coursework, accessibility needs, and budget constraints. That's why we've designed this service to be completely free while maintaining professional-grade audio quality.

Smart Sharing for Cost Savings

Help us keep this service free! Share documents with classmates instead of everyone uploading the same file.
Why Share Documents?
Save Costs

Each audio generation costs us money. When multiple students upload the same document, we generate duplicate audio files. Sharing means one high-quality audio serves everyone.

Faster Access

Shared documents are processed once and become immediately available to all authorized users. No waiting for duplicate processing.

How Sharing Works
  1. Upload Once: One student uploads the document and waits for processing
  2. Share the Link: The uploader shares their document with classmates
  3. Generate Together: All students can generate audio from the same document
  4. Everyone Benefits: Same audio quality, faster access, lower costs

Example: If 10 students in a class all need audio for the same textbook chapter:

  • ❌ Without sharing: 10 separate uploads = 10× processing cost
  • ✅ With sharing: 1 upload + 9 shares = 1× processing cost

Technical Details

Backend
  • Django 5.2 (Python web framework)
  • PostgreSQL database
  • Redis for caching & task queue
  • Celery for background processing
AI & Cloud Services
  • AWS Polly (text-to-speech)
  • AWS S3 (file storage)
  • PyMuPDF (PDF processing)
  • pandoc (document conversion)

Security: All processing happens server-side with enterprise-grade security. Files are encrypted, access is logged, and sensitive data is never stored permanently.
